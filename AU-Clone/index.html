Important Instructions for Registration

  • Applicants must check the eligibility for each programme in the admission brochure available on this website as well as on the University’s website (Admission 2025 - University of Allahabad) before registering.
  • Admission to all UG Programs of the University for the session 2025-2026 will be done through this portal only based on the CUET (UG) – 2025 score.
  • Before Registration/Profile update, applicants must ensure they have the following documents and meet the eligibility criteria prescribed by the University of Allahabad:
    • CUET-UG-2025 admit card
    • CUET-UG-2025 score card
    • Class 10 Marksheet cum Certificate / Marksheet & Certificate
    • Class 10+2 Marksheet cum Certificate / Marksheet & Certificate
    • Photo & signature of applicant in jpeg/jpg format
    • Recent caste certificate in central government format for EWS/OBC/SC/ST bearing certificate numbers and certificate issuance date
  • Applicants can register for either a single programme or multiple programmes of their choice. The non-refundable registration fee for respective social categories is as follows:
    • UR/OBC-NCL/EWS ₹ 300.00 (Rupees Three Hundred only)
    • SC/ST/PwD ₹ 150.00 (Rupees One Hundred Fifty only)
  • The applicants can select course(s)/programme on the portal. Registration fee (₹300 for UR/OBC/EWS and ₹150 for SC/ST/PwD) must be paid for each selected course/programme.
  • Only applicants who have successfully registered/updated their profile and paid the registration fee after programme/course selection will be considered for counseling.
  • The registration fee mentioned is for one programme only. For multiple programmes, applicants must pay a separate registration fee for each programme chosen.
  • Registration will be considered complete only after successful remittance of the registration fee.
  • Applicants must ensure that personal details such as name, father’s name, mother’s name, and DoB match exactly with the 10th (High School) passing certificate.
  • AADHAAR and ABC ID are mandatory for all applicants seeking admission to any programme at the University of Allahabad.
